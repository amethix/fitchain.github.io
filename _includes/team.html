Team


The fitchain team is formed by experts from multidisciplinary environments, such as big data engineering, artificial intelligence and blockchain technology. Their founders have multiple years of experience in technology, entrepreneurship and business.


Francesco Gadaleta PhD

Francesco Gadaleta has more than 15 years experience in computer programming and algorithm design. He graduated in computer engineering from Politecnico di Milano, Italy where he developed novel analytical methods to detect breast cancer (BRCA1/2).

He has a PhD in Computer Science from University KU Leuven where he designed novel security techniques with virtualization technology (hypervisor). He was member of the advanced analytics team of Johnson&Johnson.

He currently hosts podcast datascienceathome.com, the podcast that "makes artificial intelligence easy for everyone".
Francesco graduated as Techstars Alumni, participating to the Techstars Accelerator at Barclays 2017 in Cape Town, South Africa, as the Chief Data Officer of startup Abe.ai

Daan Gerits

Daan Gerits has more than 13 years of software engineering experience, with a major focus on big data and big data engineering.

He has grown into a technology leader, pragmatic and hands-on data architect and tinkerer responsible for the design and implentation of big data architectures at multiple telecommunication operators, service providers and public broadcasters.

In 2011 he started bigdata.be together with 7 partners, growing the initiative to a community of thousands of data enthousiasts. In 2014 he co-founded bigboards.io, a hardware/software startup building desktop-sized clusters for data analytics
